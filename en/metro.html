Le Métropolitain

design and HTML/CSS · November 2019

For my first school project at Gobelins on the subject "everyday object", I decided to create a tribute website to the Parisian metro ticket, which is going to disappear in 2020. I researched and organized the content, imagined the design of the website (inspired by the visual codes of the Parisian metro and the company that runs it), created the assets on Adobe Illustrator, and wrote the HTML/CSS.
